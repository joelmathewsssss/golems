# Golems Game
The golems of the City of Ankh Morpork are rising up! Led by a human named Adora,
they have finally had enough long hours and no pay. The city’s police force, the City
Watch, is going to try to intervene. The bad news is that the golems are nearly invincible.
The good news is that the golems are controlled by small spells that are put inside their
heads. If the Watch can determine the right combination then they will be able to return
the golems to a docile state.
The control spells are made up of a sequence of 4 elements selected from the following:
Fire, Water, Air, and Earth. Duplicates are allowed. The City Watch will have to try a
series of spells in hopes of disabling the golems. Fortunately, the golems have eyes that
will glow in a way that will give the Watch some clues. Their eyes will glow RED for each
element that is in the correct location. Their eyes will glow BLUE for each element that is
correctly included, but in the wrong location.
For example, if the secret code is FFAW (Fire, Fire, Air, Water) and the Watch guesses
FEWA, then the eyes will glow RBB. If they were to then guess FAFW, then the eyes will
glow RRBB. The Watch will get 10 guesses only....
Components:
● Randomly generated secret spell code ( look up rand() )
● Robust interface to enter the guesses. Only valid guesses will count, be accommodating
in terms of case.
● Limit the number of guesses
